Common Gun Issues

Understanding common firearm issues helps you stay safe and avoid range downtime. Here are a few we see frequently:

  • Failure to feed rounds properly: Often caused by dirty magazines or worn springs. Regular cleaning and inspection solve this.
  • Misfires or light primer strikes: Can result from faulty ammo or firing pin issues. Always check ammo quality and have a gunsmith inspect if needed.
  • Jammed magazines: Dirt or improper loading can cause jams. Clean and rotate magazines regularly.
  • Rust or corrosion: Exposure to moisture leads to rust. Apply protective oils and store guns dry.
  • Loose sights or optics: Vibrations and handling can loosen screws. Tighten regularly and check before shooting.

For all of these, our services can help keep your firearm in top condition.
